INTRODUCTION

Our project is about a system which will extinguish the fire by
taking the minimum time.The purpose of our project is to save
water by using fire extinguishing bomb in place of water and
avoiding human injuries or suffocation of any living being causing
death. To rescue the people without harming anything. Using IoT & 5G for
faster communication services.

PROJECT

Problem:-

Nowadays, there is water scarcity in the world and many buildings
are catching fire and normally, they are using water sprinklers for
extinguishing fire where a lot of water is wasted with the wastage
of time. But if the fire is caused due to petroleum or shot
circuit, we cannot use water sprinklers as a common extinguisher.
For that reason now a day’s people are using fire extinguishing
bomb for extinguishing fire but it is very harmful as it contains
compressed carbon dioxide gas and if a person inhales this gas he/she
may die. But there is no system made till now which can launch the
bomb automatically towards the fire at the minimum time; using the
maximum speed to transmit the data to the necessary addresses like
the fire station, neighboring area, family members; to prevent the
human beings from injury or suffocation.

Solution:-

In our system, there will be a safety mask and safety glass thrower.
When the fire sensor will sense the fire, the mask and glass thrower
will be activated and throw the masks and safety glass in all the
direction so that the people can wear it and be safe. But when there
will be nobody in the room and fire is caused by any source, the
masks, glasses will be wasted and time will be wasted so the system
will not throw the safety masks and glasses only if there is no person
sensor (this sensor will be attached near the door to count the
incoming person number and the outgoing person number to calculate the
number of people in the room). And, after the people who are present
in the room wear the masks and glasses, the bomb-thrower will throw
the bomb. To drop the bomb in the perfect place we are using fire tracker.
And, after that, the laser light exit signal will be given to the people
present in the room. After that our system will release oxygen gas to
maintain the amount of oxygen in the room and not to give any chance of
suffocation of any person in the room with the permission of the owner or
relative of the owner of the house or room through our app which will show
that is the fire is extinguished or still there because if the fire will be there then the oxygen will help to spread the fire, and this all will be shown by the small camera which will be there in the system. And with the help of Node MCU, the message will be shown in the Google notes. So that the people who will be using Google can come and help. And with the help of Global System for Mobile (GSM) Module, the message (fire has been caught in the particular location. Come for Help.) Will be sent to the Fire Station.

FIRE EXTINGUISHING BOMB Concept:-

Our carbon dioxide bomb will be made of glass which will store compressed carbon dioxide gas and powder. The glass ball will be then coated with a layer of plastics wilt a lot of holes in it so that the gas can come out when the bomb falls down and glass breaks. We are coating it with plastic so that the glass doesn't go into anyone's body.

Special Features:-

  3. Novel Idea(We have researched for similar Ideas and patents in different websites by giving keywords and we have not got a single idea or patent which is similar to our idea).

  5. Automatic control.
  6. Accurate tracking of fire.
  7. Maintains the amount of oxygen in the room after the explosion of Fire Extinguishing Bomb.

  9. App controlled.

Advantages:-

  1. Fire will be automatically extinguished in less time with Fire Extinguishing Bomb.
  2. The fire can be tracked so that the Fire Extinguishing Bomb will fall in the accurate place.
  3. The system will maintain the amount of oxygen in the room.
